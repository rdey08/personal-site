Built at the Desert Dev Lab hackathon: a progressive web app that uses Gemini
Vision to identify pantry items, estimate expiry dates, and suggest recipes
before food goes to waste. It promotes local produce, generates personalized
meal plans, and tracks food waste to encourage sustainability.
